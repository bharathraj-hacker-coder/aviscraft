    CARBON FIBER REINFORCEMENTS All of our carbon fibre cloth is premium quality fabric woven in our facility suited for manufacturers. We maintain full traceability from the loom to your delivery and our careful handling and packing ensure you receive your carbon fibre fabric in perfect condition.
    Our range of woven carbon fibre fabrics includes carbon cloth in a number of weave styles, tow sizes, weights and widths, in all cases made from premium high strength fibre. Twill and Plain type weave are regular Carbon Bidirectional fabric weave types. Contact our technical team if you need help or advice choosing the right woven reinforcement for your application.

    GLASS FIBER REINFORCEMENTS All woven glass fabrics within this category are suitable for processing by all standard methods including wet-lay, vacuum bag and resin infusion and using all typical resin systems including epoxy, polyester and vinylester. Woven glass fabric can be used on its own as the reinforcement for fibreglass/GRP moulds or components, or can be combined with other types of reinforcement such as carbon fibre or synthetic fibres to produce a laminate with particular properties.
    Chopped strand mat is the classic reinforcement for traditional 'hand layup' fibreglassing jobs, especially together with polyester laminating resin.

    WOVEN ARAMID / KEVLAR CLOTG REINFORCEMENTS Aramid fibres are very high tenacity man-made fibres with excellent mechanical properties in tensile strength and especially in elongation-to-break, they also have extremely high resistance to heat, impact and abrasion.
    Kevlar cloth is frequently used in laminates for motorsport, canoes, kayaks, ballistics and much more. This dry Kevlar® fabric is suitable for use with most composite lamination methods including hand-layup, vacuum bagging and resin infusion. To ensure a good bond between the matrix and the aramid fibre, epoxy resin is recommended for use with aramid reinforcements.

    PACKAGING All our reinforcement fabrics are Wound on cardboard rolls, sealed in polyethylene bag, and placed in cardboard box. Rolls stacked horizontally on pallets when shipping. Our fabrics are typically available in the form of rolls with various roll width roll length from 25 m up to 100 m.
    STORAGE It should be stored in its original packaging in a dry and cool place. Best conditions are at temperature from 10 to 35°C and humidity between 35 and 85%.
