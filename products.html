    Advanced Composites
    Carbon Fiber Composites

    Carbon fibre composites are used in a range of high performance applications and is recognised for its excellent strength and stiffness compared to its weight.

    Carbon Fiber Laminates

    Carbon fibre laminates finds itself in the field of robotics, UAV, drone structures, motorsport, marine, automation and medical fields. Laminates are specially developed to meet the most stringent repair and retrofitting standards.

    Fiber Reinforced Polymer Composites

    Composites are also made with fibres such as glass, Kevlar and other natural fibres. The use of these composite materials provide engineers the ability to tailor the combination of fibres and resin to meet the design requirement and performance.

    Technical Fabrics & Yarns
    Reinforcement Fabrics

    Our range of woven reinforcement are lightweight yet sturdy with good fatigue and impact resistance. They are suitable for processing by all standard methods including wet-lay, vacuum bag and resin infusion and using all typical resin systems including epoxy, polyester and vinylester.

    Coated and Laminated Fabrics

    Coated and laminated fabrics are typically woven, knitted or non-woven textile fabrics which are combined with a thin, flexible film of a natural or synthetic polymeric substance to achieve desired property.

    Spun Yarn

    Wide range of Technical yarns and sewing threads for high performance applications are made from synthetic and natural fibres.
