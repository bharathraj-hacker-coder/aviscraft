    In the textile industry, the lamination and wicking processes are performed to obtain fabrics whose finishes contribute to their specific and functional technical properties. We manufacture wide range of coated fabrics based on clients requirements and applications.
    FIRE RESISTANT FABRIC FOR FOUNDRIES These technical fabrics are designed for protection from radiant heat and molten metal splashes. These flame retardant fabrics are developed to protect exposed workers against radiant heat sources and to protect against splashes of molten metal, so they are fabrics specially designed for iron foundries, aluminum foundries, zinc smelters, and cement plants.
    ANTI MICROBIAL FABRICS An antimicrobial fabric is any textile that offers protection against bacteria, mold, mildew, and other hazardous microbes. This can be achieved by treating the fabric with a proprietary topical chemical that inhibits the growth of pathogens.
